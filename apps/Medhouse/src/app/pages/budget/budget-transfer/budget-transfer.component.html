Transfer Guidelines
  • Ensure sufficient balance is available in the source budget before initiating transfer
  • Transfer amount must be greater than zero
  • Source and destination budgets must be different
  • Provide a clear description explaining the reason for the transfer
  • All transfers are logged and can be tracked for audit purposes
  • Transfers cannot be undone, but reverse transfers can be created if needed
